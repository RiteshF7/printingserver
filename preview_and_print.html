üìã Printing Instructions:

  1. Load PDF(s): Click "Select PDF(s)" and choose one or multiple PDF files. Multiple files will be automatically merged with even page logic (blank pages added when needed).
  2. Print Even Pages: Click "Print Even Pages" - In the print dialog, enter the even page range (e.g., 2,4,6,8... or 2-240:2 for every 2nd page)
  3. Flip Pages: After printing, flip the entire stack of printed pages
  4. Print Odd Pages: Click "Print Odd Pages" when ready - In the print dialog, enter the odd page range (e.g., 1,3,5,7... or 1-240:2 for every 2nd page starting from 1)
  5. Done: Your double-sided document is ready!
