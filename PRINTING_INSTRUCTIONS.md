# Printing Instructions

## Batch Files
- **Batch_1.pdf** - 20 pages
- **Batch_2.pdf** - 12 pages

## Manual Duplex Printing Steps

### For Batch_1.pdf (20 pages total)

1. **Print the fronts (pages 1-10 only)**
   - Print pages 1-10 from Batch_1.pdf
   - Stack printed pages face-down in order

2. **Flip the entire stack**
   - Turn the stack over (same order)
   - Put back in printer tray

3. **Print the backs (pages 11-20 only)**
   - Print pages 11-20 from Batch_1.pdf on the reverse side
   - Backs are already rotated 180° in the PDF

### For Batch_2.pdf (12 pages total)

1. **Print pages 1-6** (fronts)
2. **Flip the stack**
3. **Print pages 7-12** (backs)

## Notes
- Each batch PDF contains fronts (first half) and backs (second half)
- Fronts are already reversed in the PDF
- Backs are already rotated 180° in the PDF
- Print first half → flip stack → print second half
